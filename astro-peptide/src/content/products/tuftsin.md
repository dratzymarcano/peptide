## Tuftsin — 5 mg, ≥98 % HPLC — research overview

Tuftsin is a tetrapeptide (Thr-Lys-Pro-Arg) derived from the CH2 domain of IgG heavy chain. It activates macrophages, monocytes and granulocytes and is a classical reference for phagocytosis and innate-immunity research.

Peptide Shop supplies Tuftsin (1 × 5 mg vial) as a sealed research material with lot-level documentation. This product is supplied for in-vitro laboratory research only and is not intended for human use, veterinary use, therapy, diagnosis, cosmetic application or consumption.

## Research applications

- Macrophage and monocyte activation research models
- Phagocytosis and respiratory-burst assays
- Innate-immunity and host-defence research
- Vaccine-adjuvant peptide research

## Sequence and analytical data

Identity confirmed by ESI-MS ([M+H]+ = 501.3). Highly water-soluble.

Store lyophilised at -20 °C. Aqueous stock solutions are stable for several days at 4 °C.
